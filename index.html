A Little About Me

I've always enjoyed creating. As a child, I made drawings (scribbles?) for anyone who would take them. As time went on, my skill with pencil and paper improved. In high school, I became interested in computer programming, attracted by the ability to create new things with new tools.

While I appreciate all formats of programming, I felt drawn to web development. I enjoy the complexity that comes with full stack development: designing a visually appealing front end, and designing a streamlined back end.

Lately, I've been doing a lot of work with custom ERP systems. Those artistic skills I developed turned out to be a good asset when it comes to making interfaces that people need to stare at through their work day. I take pride in my work and look for inspiriation everywhere.

I keep busy and pick up new skills along the way.

I still draw from time to time.
